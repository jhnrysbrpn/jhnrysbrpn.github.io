Los Angeles Lakers

The Los Angeles Lakers are an American professional basketball team based
in Los Angeles. The Lakers compete in the National Basketball Association (NBA)
as a member of the Pacific Division of the Western Conference. The Lakers are one of
the most successful teams in the history of the NBA, and have won 17 NBA
championships, tied with the Boston Celtics for the most in NBA history.
